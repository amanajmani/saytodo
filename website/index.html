How it works

Three seconds to organized

No forms, no clicks, no friction. Just hold a key and speak.

1

Hold Shift & Speak

Hold Shift anywhere on your Mac (or press Cmd+Shift+Space). Say your task naturally, in English or Hindi.

2

AI Does the Rest

Our AI cleans filler words, detects priority, extracts dates, picks a category, and translates Hindi to English.

3

Done. Organized.

Your clean task appears instantly with priority, due date, and category badges. Right there in your menubar.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built for speed and simplicity. Every feature earned its place.

Voice-First Capture

Hold Shift and speak. Or press Cmd+Shift+Space. Your voice becomes an organized task in seconds.

AI Smart Parsing

Groq-powered AI removes filler words, detects priority, extracts dates, and categorizes tasks — all in one call.

Voice Commands

"Mark groceries done", "delete the report task", "change dentist to high priority". Full control without touching the mouse.

Recurring Tasks

"Gym every Monday Wednesday Friday" — it creates repeating tasks that auto-spawn each morning. Build habits.

Voice Daily Briefing

Say "what's my day look like" and hear your tasks read aloud. Start every morning with clarity.

Voice Notes

"Add note to dentist task — bring insurance card." Attach context to any task without opening the app.

English + Hindi + Hinglish

Speak in English, Hindi, or mix both. "Kal grocery leni hai" becomes "Buy groceries" with a Tomorrow label. The AI translates and cleans everything into English tasks.

Menubar Quick Glance

Right-click the menubar icon to see today's tasks at a glance. Click to toggle done. No window needed.
