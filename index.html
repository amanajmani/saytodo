Microphone Access Needed

SayTodo needs microphone and speech recognition permission to capture voice tasks.

1. System Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone
2. System Settings > Privacy & Security > Speech Recognition
3. System Settings > Keyboard > Dictation > turn ON
4. System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility
